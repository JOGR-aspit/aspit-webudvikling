Build Your Universe, One Star at a Time

Explore the cosmos, gather resources from distant planets, and construct the ultimate space station. Stellar Forge is an upcoming sandbox simulation game where your creativity shapes the galaxy.

Features

  • Procedural Universe: Explore infinite procedurally generated solar systems, each with unique planets to discover
  • Deep Building System: Construct modular space stations with over 200 different building pieces and components
  • Resource Management: Mine asteroids, harvest gas giants, and manage complex supply chains
  • Co-op Multiplayer: Build and explore together with friends in shared universes
  • Living Economy: Trade with alien factions, influence galactic markets, and build trade empires
