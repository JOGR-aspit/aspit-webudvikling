The Kitchen

Investigating Rose

Behind the Scenes

The kitchen is warm and smells of fresh bread. Copper pots hang from the ceiling, and a large wooden table sits in the center. Rose, the young maid, is kneading dough. She looks up nervously when you enter.

"Oh! You must be the detective," she says, wiping her hands on her apron. "I... I already told Mrs. Blackwood everything I know. I was cleaning the hallway outside the master bedroom yesterday afternoon, but I never went inside."

Questioning Rose

You ask Rose about her movements yesterday. She thinks carefully before answering.

"I finished cleaning the hallway around 3 o'clock," she says. "Then I came down here to start preparing dinner. I was in the kitchen until after 6 o'clock. You can ask Mr. Grayson - he came in several times for tea."

You notice her hands are trembling slightly. Is she hiding something, or just nervous about being questioned?

An Interesting Discovery

As you look around the kitchen, something catches your eye. On the windowsill, there's a bowl of cat treats. Beside it is a note in Rose's handwriting:

"Mittens was very active yesterday - running up and down the stairs all afternoon. Must be the new cat toy I gave her. Hope she's not getting into trouble!"

Rose sees you reading the note. "Oh, that's right! Mittens loves to explore. She's always carrying things around. Last week she stole my favorite hair ribbon and hid it under the sofa!"

Clues Discovered

  1. Rose's alibi - She was in the kitchen from 3-6 PM
  2. Mittens the cat - Known for stealing and hiding small objects
  3. Cat activity - Was seen running up and down stairs all afternoon

What Do You Do?

  • Accuse Rose of the theft - She was near the bedroom!
  • Investigate the garden - Mittens might have hidden the brooch outside
  • Go to the library - Check what Mr. Grayson knows
