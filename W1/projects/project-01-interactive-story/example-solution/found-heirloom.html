The Truth Revealed

You carefully lift the loose board at the back of the shed and squeeze inside. The shed is dusty and filled with old gardening tools. But there, in the corner, you spot something that makes your heart race.

A small pile of treasures lies on an old shelf. Hair ribbons, shiny buttons, a silver spoon... and there, gleaming in the dim light, is Mrs. Blackwood's diamond brooch!

A Happy Ending

You return to the house, brooch in hand. Mrs. Blackwood gasps when she sees it. "My grandmother's brooch! You found it!" She clutches it to her chest, tears of joy in her eyes.

"But who took it?" she asks.

You smile. "No person took it, Mrs. Blackwood. Mittens the cat has been collecting treasures. She must have found the brooch sparkling in the sunlight and carried it off to her secret hiding place."

Mittens, who has been watching from the doorway, lets out an innocent "meow" and begins grooming her paw as if nothing happened.

Your Detective Skills

Great job! You solved the case by:

  1. Gathering clues from multiple locations
  2. Listening to witnesses without jumping to conclusions
  3. Following the evidence - paw prints led you to the truth
  4. Not accusing the innocent suspects

Mrs. Blackwood pays you handsomely for your services. As you drive away from Blackwood Manor, you feel the satisfaction of another case well solved.
